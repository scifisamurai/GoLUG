What is GoLUG?

GoLUG (The Greater Orlando Linux Users Group) is an organization for people in the greater Orlando Florida area with an interest in the GNU/Linux Operating System. Whether you're new to Linux, been using it for a bit, or can write your own kernel driver you're welcome in GoLUG.

We have monthly meetings where we get together to discuss a Linux related topic. Members also correspond regularly via our mailing list and IRC. If you're interested in Linux and want to find like minded individuals join us.
